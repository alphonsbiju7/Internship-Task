About HackSphere

HackSphere 2025 is a 48-hour global online hackathon that brings together developers, designers, and creators from around the world to innovate and build solutions to real-world challenges.

Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned hacker, HackSphere is your platform to learn, collaborate, and showcase your creativity to a global audience.
